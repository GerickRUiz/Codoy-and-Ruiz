ABOUT WEIGHT MASTERS

The Ideal Weight Calculator computes ideal bodyweight (IBW) ranges based on height, gender, and age. The idea of finding the IBW using a formula has been sought after by many experts for a long time. These experts are Robinson, Miller, Hamwi, and Devine. This program will also help you track how heavy you are for your age or if your weight is suitable for you age.
